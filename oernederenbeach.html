winter bathing

Aarhus
Ørnereden beach

Experience level: experienced adventurers

Natural, wild spot to enjoy a beautiful beach framed by tall trees, while easily reachable by bus. It is just a wild beach, so taking the dip takes some getting used to! Also the lack of facilities like changing cabins or toilets requires you to come prepared to fully enjoy the more natural experience. However the nearby kiosk offers drinks and food!
Facilities & What to bring
Facilities:
  • A kiosk with drinks and food nearby
Bring along:
  • Towels and maybe a cozy bathrobe
  • Maybe some swim shows to protect your feet from sharp stones
  • A hot drink for the warm up after
  • Dry clothes to change into
Reviews
How to get there
Take the bus line 31 stop Ørnereden/Ørneredevej, past the kiosk and down the stairs, the beach is right around the corner! Use the marker on the map to find a specific route!
