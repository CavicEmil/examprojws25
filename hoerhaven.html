winter bathing

Aarhus
Hørhaven bathing bridge

Experience level: casual, experienced bathers

Beautiful, private spot to enjoy some quiet and solitude, while easily reachable by bus. The lack of facilities like changing cabins or toilets requires you to come prepared to fully enjoy the more natural experience.
Facilities & What to bring
Facilities:
  • Bathing bridge
Bring along:
  • Towels and maybe a cozy bathrobe
  • A hot drink for the warm up after
  • Dry clothes to change into
Reviews
How to get there
Take the bus line 31 stop Høraven/Ørneredevej, its a 10 minute walk from there. Click the marker on the map to find a specific route!
