winter bathing

Aarhus
Ballehage Strand

Experience level: beginner friendly!

Convenient spot close to town with good facilities closeby with a nice view of Aarhus!
Facilities & What to bring
Facilities:
  • Changing cabins
  • Bathing bridge
  • Toilet closeby
Bring along:
  • Towels and maybe a cozy bathrobe
  • A hot drink for the warm up after
  • Dry clothes to change into
Reviews
How to get there
Take the bus line 31, 100, 103 or 302 and have a short walk through the forest or along the beach. Click the marker on the map for a personal route for you.
